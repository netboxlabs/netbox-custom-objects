GraphQL Cache Clearer

This tool helps clear GraphiQL's cached schema data.

Instructions:

  1. Click "Clear GraphQL Cache" to clear all cached data
  2. Click "Test NetBox GraphQL" to verify the current schema
  3. Click "Open GraphiQL" to open the GraphiQL interface
  4. In GraphiQL, press Ctrl+Shift+R (or Cmd+Shift+R on Mac) to hard refresh
